About Us

Town Planner Connect is a modern web platform designed to bring communities, residents, and planners together in one place. Our goal is to make city and town planning more transparent, collaborative, and accessible. We provide tools to share development projects, gather public feedback, and connect people with local planning initiatives. By combining technology with community needs, Town Planner Connect helps create smarter, sustainable, and people-friendly towns.
